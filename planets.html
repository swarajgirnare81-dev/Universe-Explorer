🪐 Planets Explorer

Explore the amazing planets of our Solar System.

☿ Mercury

☀️ Position: 1st planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 0
🌡️ Temperature: Extreme hot and cold
⭐ Fact: Mercury is the smallest planet in our Solar System.

♀ Venus

☀️ Position: 2nd planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 0
🌡️ Temperature: Hottest planet
⭐ Fact: Venus rotates in the opposite direction to most planets.

🌍 Earth

☀️ Position: 3rd planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 1
🌡️ Temperature: Suitable for life
⭐ Fact: Earth is the only known planet with life.

🔴 Mars

☀️ Position: 4th planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 2
🌡️ Temperature: Cold desert world
⭐ Fact: Mars is called the Red Planet.

🟠 Jupiter

☀️ Position: 5th planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 90+
🌡️ Temperature: Very cold
⭐ Fact: Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.

💍 Saturn

☀️ Position: 6th planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 140+
🌡️ Temperature: Very cold
⭐ Fact: Saturn has the most famous ring system.

🩵 Uranus

☀️ Position: 7th planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 27+
🌡️ Temperature: One of the coldest planets
⭐ Fact: Uranus rotates on its side.

🔵 Neptune

☀️ Position: 8th planet from the Sun
🌙 Moons: 14+
🌡️ Temperature: Extremely cold
⭐ Fact: Neptune has the fastest winds in the Solar System.

🌞 Solar System

Our Solar System contains the Sun, eight planets, moons, asteroids and comets. It is located in the Milky Way galaxy.
