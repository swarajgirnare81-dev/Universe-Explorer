Journey through the wonders of the Universe.

☀️ The Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of our Solar System. It provides light and heat that make life on Earth possible.

🪐 Solar System

Our Solar System contains the Sun, eight planets, dwarf planets, moons, asteroids and comets.

🌌 Milky Way Galaxy

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System. It has hundreds of billions of stars.

⭐ Stars

Stars are giant balls of hot gases that produce their own light and energy through nuclear fusion.

☄️ Comets

Comets are icy objects that orbit the Sun and develop bright tails when they approach it.

🛰️ Artificial Satellites

Satellites orbit Earth and other planets to help with communication, navigation, weather forecasting and scientific research.

🚀 Space Missions

Space agencies such as NASA, ISRO and ESA explore space using rockets, satellites, probes and astronauts.
