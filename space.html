☀️ The Sun

The Sun is the center of our Solar System. It provides light and energy that make life on Earth possible.

🌌 Galaxies

A galaxy is a huge collection of stars, gas and dust held together by gravity. The Milky Way is our home galaxy.

⭐ Stars

Stars are giant balls of hot gas that produce their own light and energy through nuclear fusion.

🕳️ Black Holes

Black holes are regions of space with extremely strong gravity where even light cannot escape.

☄️ Comets & Asteroids

Comets are icy objects that travel around the Sun, while asteroids are rocky objects found mostly in the asteroid belt.

🛰️ Satellites

Artificial satellites help with communication, GPS, weather prediction and scientific research.

🚀 Space Agencies

🌎 NASA — United States
🇮🇳 ISRO — India
🇪🇺 ESA — Europe

They explore space using rockets, spacecraft and telescopes.

🌠 Amazing Space Facts

✨ There are billions of galaxies in the universe.
✨ Light from distant stars takes years to reach Earth.
✨ Space is constantly expanding.
