🌍 Beautiful Earth

Earth is the only known planet that supports life. It has oceans, forests, deserts, mountains, millions of living species and a protective atmosphere.

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. Around 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, while the remaining 29% is land. It is home to millions of species, including humans.

🌎 Quick Facts

  • 🌍 Planet Number: 3rd from the Sun
  • 📏 Diameter: 12,742 km
  • 🌊 Water Coverage: 71%
  • 🌎 Land Coverage: 29%
  • 🌙 Moon: 1 Natural Satellite
  • ☀️ Distance from Sun: 149.6 million km
  • 🕒 One Day: 24 Hours
  • 📅 One Year: 365.25 Days

🌊 Earth's Oceans

Pacific Ocean, Atlantic Ocean, Indian Ocean, Southern Ocean and Arctic Ocean together cover most of Earth's surface.

Mount Everest, Amazon Rainforest, Sahara Desert, Grand Canyon, Himalayan Mountains and many more amazing places.
