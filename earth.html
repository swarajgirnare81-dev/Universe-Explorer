🌍 About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. It has oceans, continents, mountains, forests and millions of species.

🌊 Oceans

Earth has five major oceans: Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern and Arctic. Oceans cover about 71% of Earth's surface.

🏔️ Mountains

Mount Everest is the highest mountain above sea level. Mountain ranges shape Earth's landscapes and ecosystems.

🌋 Volcanoes

Volcanoes are openings in Earth's crust where magma, gases and ash can escape. They help create new land.

🌲 Forests & Wildlife

Forests are home to millions of plants and animals. Rainforests play an important role in Earth's climate.

🌦️ Climate

Earth has different climates including tropical, desert, polar and temperate regions.

📊 Earth Facts

🌍 Age: About 4.5 billion years
🌙 Moon: 1 natural satellite
☀️ Distance from Sun: About 150 million km
🌡️ Average temperature: About 15°C
